Digital illustration

Using digital tools to produce art directly by the artist. Usually done with tools such as drawing tablets or stylises which are made to parallel natural drawing methods.

Digital Painting

An emerging art form converting traditional painting techniques and applied using digital methods/ tools by means of a computer/ other electronic and a drawing software.

Digital Literature

A genre of literature made specifically to be consumed digitally, usually integrating unigilly digital elements that cannot be printed or converted into a non digital format.

Digital sculpture

A form of 3D computer graphics in which software is used to render 3D models, a mathematical representation of any three dimensional object.

Internet/ Interactive art New

A form of new media art, usually involving interaction with the art itself which allows it to circumnavigate the traditional use of physical gallery type systems. Intrinsically involving the use of digital interface in some element of the art.

Photo manipulation Popular

Transformation or alteration of a photograph using varius editing methods to achieve a certain effect.

Texture Mapping

A method for defining high frequency surface texture or color information onto a 3D model.
